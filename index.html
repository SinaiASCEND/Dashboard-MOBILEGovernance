Governance Dashboard

A reshaped mobile experience for the EEC governance app — focused on the one thing a phone user needs: getting to a meeting and its minutes fast.

The flow

  1. Home — one hero button for the Office of Curricular Affairs (all meetings), plus a 2×2 grid of committees: EEC, PCCS, CCS, AES.
  2. Meetings — tap a committee to see its meetings, each row showing the date and a few agenda bullets.
  3. Meeting detail — four big buttons: Meeting Summary · Governance Actions · Operational Actions · Download Minutes.
